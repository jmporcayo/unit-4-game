Crystal Collector!
You will be given a random number at the start of the game.
There are four crystals below. By clicking on a crystal you will add a specific amount of points to you total score.
You win the game by matching your total score toa random number, you lose the game if your toal score goes above the random number.
The value of each crystal is hidden from younuntil you click on it.
